About

PATHA TRUST

Welcome to Project PATHA, a pioneering initiative dedicated to bridging the gap between underprivileged individuals and compassionate individuals like you willing to support them. At PATHA, we believe in creating better opportunities and pathways for marginalised communities, especially children facing socio-economic challenges. With a deep understanding of societal needs and a passion for social impact, Ms. Jeevitha B Ko ng envisioned PATHA as a catalyst for positive change. Backed by a team of compassionate individuals and strategic partners, we are here to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To bridge the gap between underprivileged individuals and organisations willing to support them, and to create better opportunities for marginalised communities.

Vision

A society where everyone has equal access to resources, support, and opportunities for growth, regardless of their socio-economic background.

THE CAUSE

In our country, there's a significant divide between underprivileged individuals and the support they require to thrive, as well as individuals like you and me who are willing to offer assistance but lack direction on where and how to help. This gap spans across age, education, healthcare, economic opportunities, social support, environmental justice and so on.
Patha's mission is to bridge this gap by being a guiding light and driving force for positive change. We acknowledge the hurdles faced by marginalised communities and are dedicated to addressing them through collaborative efforts with compassionate individuals and corporations.

THE GAP

Numerous systemic challenges persist among underprivileged communities, including limited access to education, healthcare, economic opportunities, social support, technology, environmental justice, exacerbating inequalities and hindering overall well-being.

WHAT WE DO

WHAT WE DO

1. Raising Awareness: We raise awareness about available resources, support services, and opportunities for underprivileged individuals and communities.

2. Access to Resources: Our platform provides a centralised hub where beneficiaries can easily access information about available resources and support services.

3. Coordinated Support: We bridge fragmented support systems by connecting beneficiaries with a wide range of support services and resources.

4. Networking Opportunities: We provide networking opportunities for beneficiaries to connect with individuals, organisations, and resources for growth.

5. Data and Impact Measurement: We implement robust monitoring and evaluation mechanisms to track the progress and impact of our programs, providing valuable insights for stakeholders.

OUR PROJECTS

We have the right package for you

1. Patha Sethu

(Bridging the Gap Between service givers and individuals in need)

Project Sethu endeavors to facilitate connections between underprivileged individuals and service givers. We meticulously analyze the requirements of the individual in need and subsequently facilitate connections with the appropriate service provider. Through this platform, individuals in need can avail themselves of information regarding available opportunities, while organizations can identify suitable beneficiaries to extend their support.
